Part B – Create a Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist: 
✅ Responsible Social Media Use Checklist (For College Students)
✔️ Do’s
•	✔️ Think before you post – ensure your content is appropriate and respectful
•	✔️ Maintain a positive and professional online image
•	✔️ Verify information before sharing (avoid fake news)
•	✔️ Use strong passwords and enable privacy settings
•	✔️ Respect others’ opinions and engage politely in discussions
________________________________________
❌ Don’ts
•	❌ Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
•	❌ Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content
•	❌ Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
•	❌ Avoid oversharing your location or daily activities
•	❌ Do not click on suspicious links or unknown messages
