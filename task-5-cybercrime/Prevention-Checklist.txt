🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)
✅ General Safety Tips
•	✔️ Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts (mix of letters, numbers, symbols)
•	✔️ Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
•	✔️ Avoid clicking on unknown links in emails, messages, or social media
•	✔️ Do not share OTP, passwords, or personal details with anyone
•	✔️ Keep your apps and devices updated to avoid security vulnerabilities
•	✔️ Download apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or App Store
•	✔️ Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi; avoid logging into sensitive accounts
________________________________________
💰 Financial / UPI Safety Tips
•	✔️ Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money (it is only used to send money)
•	✔️ Always verify the payment request (collect request) before approving
•	✔️ Do not share bank details, OTP, or UPI ID with unknown callers
•	✔️ Avoid installing screen-sharing apps on request from strangers
________________________________________
🚨 Reporting & Emergency Help
•	📞 Report fraud immediately on Helpline: 1930
•	🌐 File a complaint at National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
________________________________________
👉 Final Tip: Stay alert, think before you click, and act quickly if something seems suspicious—your awareness is your strongest defense!
